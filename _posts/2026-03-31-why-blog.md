title: "Why start a blog?"
date: 2026-03-31
---

Two reasons.

Storytelling, because I've spent enough time in rooms where the right argument lost to a better-told story. Facts inform. Stories change minds. I'd rather learn to do both, using AI for feedback until I no longer need it. 

Mentoring, because the version that works for me doesn't scale. In-person catchups over the years have helped a dozen people get promoted or find a better role. Frequent Zoom calls do the opposite of what mentoring should do, they drain the energy the conversation is supposed to create. Writing is the version that scales without that cost.

Most of what I've learned worth passing on is about developer productivity, reliability, and what actually happens when organisations make big technical changes. That's what this is.
