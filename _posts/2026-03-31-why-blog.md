date: 2026-03-31

Two reasons.

Storytelling, I've spent enough time in key decision meetings to see good arguments lose to good stories. Facts inform. Stories change minds. I want to become great at both. I will rely on AI to start this journey. My aim is barely need it after the first few posts.

Mentoring, I love it, but struggle to do it remotely. In-person catchups have helped me guide a dozen people to promotion or new opportunities. But frequent Zoom calls drain the enetgy out of me. Mentoring by writing scales without that cost.

Most of what I've learned that's worth passing on is about developer productivity, driving big technical changes, and how to engage with management. That's what this blog is about.
