title: "Why start a blog?"
date: 2026-03-31
---

My two key reasons are: practicing story-telling, and scaling mentoring beyond what's possible with just 1on1s.

On story-telling, I struggle to remember, or come up with, parables and stories. But facts and impersonal anecdotes simply don't resonate as well with people, myself included.

On mentorship, the best part of experience is helping others avoid your mistakes. Over years I've helped a dozen people get promoted or find a better job.
But the past couple of years I've reduced my mentoring a lot. In-person catchups energize me, whereas frequent Zoom meetings drain me. 

I hope that writing this blog can make me a better communicator, as well as bring back some of the joy of mentoring.
